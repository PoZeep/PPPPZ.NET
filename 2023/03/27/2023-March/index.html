Now my roommate is not here, so I have no time to play with him. I only interact with him by feeding him food and taking medicine every day. As a qualified Muppet cat, he is really clingy. I don’t need it most of the time, is it irresponsible? Is it my desire to dominate? There is a little licking cat with me, and I ignore it when I need it and ignore it when I don’t need it. Facts have proved that Dink’s idea of raising cats is not impulsive, I hate children and their noise. Hate taking care of children, why do people want to have children? In the past, it was for survival, but now this concept is gradually fading with our generation. Many people want to have a child because of the pressure at home or the boring life. Undoubtedly, it is because my parents’ disastrous marriage has given me the most intuitive feeling from childhood. I often hear my friends say that they want to escape the rules of this society, but every time they escape, they subconsciously realize that this is a bad way, which is not undesirable. Why is this? But most people are numb to live, of course in my point of view numb to live, in their world has been granted perhaps live very happy.
I don’t want to buy a car, put down roots in a city, get married or have children. I just want to buy a big villa in the suburbs and teach in a university when I get old. Now I want to learn and play games with like-minded people.
